Smart blinds that move with you.

Urban Blinds clips onto the blinds you already have and turns them into smart, solar-powered blinds in minutes. No drilling. No landlord approvals. No spending hundreds on full replacements you cannot take to your next apartment.

We designed Urban Blinds specifically for budget renters, college apartments, and frequent movers. Install is as simple as snapping on a case, pairing the app, and choosing how you want light to behave through your day. When it is time to move, you unclip the device and drop it into your next window.

Key features

Light that follows your routine.

A small device at the top of your window controls tilt, timing, and brightness—while staying almost invisible against your blinds.

Clip-on in minutes

Adjustable clips hug the headrail and wand, so install feels closer to placing a speaker on a shelf than doing a home project.

Schedules & scenes

Wake up with soft morning light, close everything for class, or set “away” scenes while you travel— all inside a simple app.

Designed for small spaces

Low-noise motors, a thin solar strip, and a neutral casing blend into your space instead of fighting with your décor.

Inside the casing

Hardware that quietly does the work.

Everything lives in a compact shell at the head of your blinds: solar, battery, motor, and brain. Nothing touches your walls or needs permanent mounting.

Solar panel strip — trickle-charges the internal Li-ion cell using everyday daylight.
Ambient light sensor — tells Urban Blinds when to open or close based on real brightness.
12V gear motor — adds quiet, torque-optimized rotation to your existing wand or chain.
ESP32 controller — runs automation, Wi-Fi/Bluetooth, and smart home integrations.
Clip-on mount + wand adapter — keeps everything renter-safe and removable in one move.
Manual button — gives you instant open/close without needing your phone nearby.
